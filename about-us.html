Who We Are

Empowering Every Drop, With 25 Years of Trust and Flow

Pentaaxx Pumps, powered by JSM Trading in Kondotty, Malappuram, Kerala, has been a trusted name in the wholesale and retail water pump industry for over 25 years. Serving homes, farms, and businesses, we have built a reputation for quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction across Kerala and beyond.

We specialize in high-performance Borewell and Openwell Submersible Pumps, designed to meet a variety of needs, from residential water supply to agricultural irrigation and industrial applications. Each pump is engineered for durability, energy efficiency, and smooth operation, ensuring consistent water flow even in challenging conditions.

At Pentaaxx, we believe a water pump is more than just a machine, it’s a lifeline. That’s why our focus is on delivering products that combine advanced technology with robust design, minimizing maintenance, saving energy, and providing peace of mind to every customer.

Our commitment doesn’t end at the sale. With prompt installation services, on-site support, and a 2-year warranty, we ensure that every Pentaaxx Pump continues to perform at its best.

For over two decades, Pentaaxx Pumps has stood for trust, consistency, and long-lasting value. Every drop powered by our pumps reflects our dedication to quality, reliability, and innovation, making us the preferred choice for homeowners, farmers, and businesses across Kerala.
